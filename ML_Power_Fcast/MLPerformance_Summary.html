Improvements to Power Forecasting for Client 1

Section 1: Summary

The client was using a “roll forward method” using power volume from 1 week + in the past and rolling forward a week to create their trade positions. This caused:

  • Errors at start of month
  • Daylight savings issues
  • Trades unresponsive to big portfolio swings
  • Intensive manual overrides for bank holidays and Christmases
